1. Medical Disclaimer

Dosenova is a wellness and organisation tool. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.

DOSENOVA IS NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE AND IS NOT INTENDED TO REPLACE THE ADVICE OF A QUALIFIED HEALTHCARE PROFESSIONAL. RELIANCE ON ANY INFORMATION PROVIDED BY THE APP IS SOLELY AT YOUR OWN RISK.

Dosenova provides general medication information and organisational tools only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

What Dosenova is

  • An organisational tool to help you manage your medication schedule
  • An informational tool that displays publicly available medication information
  • A reminder system to support — not replace — your existing medication routine
  • A communication tool to help you share information with family members

What Dosenova is not

  • A medical device
  • A substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
  • A life-safety system — caregiver alerts are a supplemental reminder only
  • A replacement for your doctor, pharmacist, or healthcare provider

Emergency situations: In a medical emergency, call 999, 911, or 112 immediately. Do not rely on this app in an emergency situation. The SOS button calls native emergency services directly — Dosenova's servers are never involved in emergency routing. Dosenova is not responsible for the failure of a device to place an emergency call due to lack of signal, battery death, OS-level restrictions, carrier issues, or any other technical failure outside our control. Always ensure your device is charged and has network coverage. The SOS feature is a convenience shortcut only — never your sole method of reaching emergency services.

AI-generated information

Dosenova uses artificial intelligence to generate medication summaries, interaction suggestions, and wellness observations. These outputs are informational only and may contain errors. They do not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. Always verify AI-generated information with your healthcare provider or pharmacist before making any health decision.

Backup reminder requirement

Dosenova recommends maintaining a physical backup of your medication schedule — such as a pillbox or written log — in case of technical issues. App notifications should not be your only reminder system.

2. Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Dosenova ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Dosenova app and website.

What data we collect

  • Medicine information — medicine names, dosages, and schedules you enter
  • Health readings — blood pressure, blood sugar, weight, and mood logs you choose to record
  • Dose history — records of doses marked taken, skipped, or missed
  • Caregiver contact details — names and contact information you provide for alerts
  • Device information — basic device type and app version for technical support

What we do NOT collect

  • Medicine scan images — these are processed in real time and never stored on our servers
  • Location data — unless you explicitly enable geofencing features
  • Voice recordings — voice check-off is processed on-device and not transmitted
  • Payment details — handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play

How we use your data

  • To provide reminders and dose tracking features
  • To send caregiver alerts when you miss a dose
  • To generate AI health insights based on your readings
  • To sync your data across devices when you enable cloud backup
  • To improve the app based on anonymised usage patterns

Data storage and security

Your data is stored locally on your device by default using encrypted storage. When cloud sync is enabled, all health data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit (TLS 1.2 or higher) and at rest on our servers. We use Supabase for cloud storage, which provides enterprise-grade security and is SOC 2 compliant.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not share your health data with advertisers, insurance companies, pharmaceutical companies, or any other commercial third parties without your explicit consent.

We may share anonymised, aggregated data (with no personally identifiable information) for research purposes. You can opt out of this at any time in Settings → Data Sharing.

Third-party services

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — processes medicine scan images via their API. Images are transmitted securely, processed in real time, and never stored after processing. Under Anthropic's standard API terms, submitted data is not used to train their AI models. Your medicine scan data is never retained by Anthropic.
  • Supabase — encrypted cloud database for optional data sync. When Cloud Sync is enabled, all health data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest.
  • OneSignal — push notification delivery. Only your device token is shared, not your health data.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon request for account deletion, all personal health data is permanently purged from our active servers within 30 days. Backups are fully purged within 90 days. Deletion is permanent and irreversible. You can request deletion at any time from Settings → Account → Delete account or by emailing dosenova.app@gmail.com.

Caregiver contact details

When you add a caregiver contact, you represent and warrant that you have obtained that person's explicit consent to receive automated alerts from Dosenova via SMS, WhatsApp, or email. By providing a caregiver's contact details, you confirm that the caregiver has agreed to receive such messages. Dosenova is not responsible for alerts sent to contacts where consent was not properly obtained.

Dosenova is not designed for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

GDPR: If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, please see Section 4 for your specific data rights under GDPR.
